Our Vision

Makhafola-Khafo seeks to be a nationally operating, customer focused organisation engaged in long term trust based relationships with its clients, through delivering a continuous professional service in the South African stationery supply, cleaning and construction industry.

Our Mission

Makhafola-Khafo strives uncompromisingly to be the preferred black owned construction company throughout South Africa.
